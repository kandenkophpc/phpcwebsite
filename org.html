Location/Project:



Fire Disaster Team

  1. Fire Chief
    Takes overall command in case of fire. Directs evacuation of personnels in case of fire.

  2. Assistant Fire Chief
    Assists the Fire Chief in all its functions. Takes over the function of the Fire Chief in case the leader is not present.

  3. Fire Pointer
    Directs the Fire Brigade to the location of fire. Accounts the members on the fire cene and takes the function of any member if not present. Reports to Fire Chief the inventory of the personnels after the fire. Checks the inventory of all fire fighting equipment at the fire.

  4. Hose man
    Unwinds properly the fire hose . Assists the Nozzle Man.

  5. Nozzle Person
    Contains the fire.

  6. Fire Hydrant Man
    Connects the hose firmly to hydrant.

  7. Fire Extinguisher Man
    Familiar with the types of fire. Familiar with the types of fire extinguishers. Knows operations of fire extinguisher.

  8. User proper PPEs

Medical

  1. Provides initial treatment or first aid.

  2. Always carry a first aid kit.

  3. Arranges for hospitalization of seriously injured person.

  4. Accompanies seriously injured person to the hospital.

Rescue

  1. Locates injured or trapped person and move them to a safe location.


Evacuation

  1. Plans personnel movement routes to evacuation area.

  2. Determines headcounts in the evacuation area.

  3. Assists and accompanies the PWDs to evacate and bringing them to a safe location.

Transportation

  1. Ensures availability of transport vehicle at al times.

  2. Ensures availability of designated driver.

  3. Facilitates transport of injured personnel to nearest clinic or hospital if necesarry.


Communication

  1. Receives emergency informations

  2. Disseminates emergency informations to all ERT members in consultation with the ERT chairman.

  3. Contacts emergency services, such as Fire Dept. Hospital, Police.

  4. Maintains appropriate communication link with ERT members

  5. Monitors emergency sitations over the radio or other means of communication.

  6. Keeps communication system open and gives priorities to emergency related calls.

  7. Refer all authorities and media representatives to designated spokesman.

  8. Records and documents events during emergency.


Security

  1. Secures the vicinity and adjacent affected areas.

  2. Controls entry of persons in the affected areas.

  3. Bring personnel incoming / outgoing report at evacuation site for head count.

  4. Establishes the inner control perimeter by setting up barricades

  5. Updates ERT Chairman regarding security matters
