About Us

Company Overview

PHPC Co., Ltd. Inc., is a member of KanDenKo Group with its principal headquarters located in Tokyo, Japan. Considered to be one of the leading electro-mechanical contractors in the Philippines, PHPC has been in active operation since 1991. As an integrated engineering company, we can offer our clients with a full range of service from conceptual designs to turnkey projects. PHPC thrives in providing our clients with excellent service stemming from the wealth in experience and competence of our Engineers and fully backed up by a team of Japanese consultants. The company strictly conforms its operations on an Integrated Management System for Quality, Environment Safety and Health (QESH), duly certified by TUV Rheinland.

Completing project after project, year after year, KanDenKo's brand equity of quality, reliability, safety, on time delivery and care for the environment has helped PHPC and its clients in fulfilling the vision of Building A Better Tomorrow, Today.

Quality, Environmental Safety & Health Policy

At PHPC Co., Ltd. Inc., we are the country's leading electrical, mechanical, and structural steel works contractor, built on a foundation of customer satisfaction. We are committed to delivering high-quality, cost-effective, and timely solutions while prioritizing environmental care, safety, and the health of our people.

We implement an integrated Quality, Environmental, Safety, and Health Management System (QESHMS) aligned with internationally recognized standards. Through this, we pledge to:

  • Provide excellent services through process-based management and evidence-driven decisions.
  • Harness employee competency through training and experience.
  • Promote safe, healthy, and eco-friendly working conditions.
  • Create strong relationships with customers and stakeholders.
  • Cultivate a culture of excellence, innovation, and integrity.
  • Optimize resource use and encourage recycling.
  • Live up to our environmental protection commitments.
  • Train and equip our workforce with safe technologies and practices.
  • Develop measurable objectives to improve QESH performance.
  • Implement waste segregation and minimize landfill disposal.
  • Network with industry leaders to adopt best practices.
  • Commit to meeting all compliance obligations across sectors.
