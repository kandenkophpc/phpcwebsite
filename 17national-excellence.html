17th National Excellence Award

April 24, 2026  |  Company Achievement
Back to News

A Milestone of Excellence and Dedication

On the 24th day of April 2026, PHPC Co., Ltd. Inc. was honored with the prestigious 17th National Excellence Award, a remarkable recognition of the company's unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and outstanding service in the electro-mechanical construction industry.

This distinguished award celebrates PHPC's continued dedication to delivering world-class engineering and construction solutions across the Philippines. As a proud member of the KANDENKO Group, the company has consistently upheld the highest standards of excellence, safety, and client satisfaction throughout its operations.

The National Excellence Award recognizes organizations that demonstrate exceptional performance, leadership, and contribution to national development. PHPC's achievement highlights its role as one of the leading electro-mechanical contractors in the country, completing complex industrial and commercial projects with precision and reliability.

"This award is a testament to the hard work and dedication of every member of the PHPC family," said company representatives. "We remain committed to our vision of Building A Better Tomorrow, Today, and we will continue to strive for excellence in every project we undertake."

The award ceremony brought together industry leaders, government officials, and partners who have collaborated with PHPC over the years. This recognition further strengthens the company's position as a trusted partner for industrial and commercial projects nationwide.

PHPC Co., Ltd. Inc. extends its gratitude to its clients, partners, and employees whose collective efforts made this achievement possible. The company looks forward to continuing its mission of delivering innovative, safe, and sustainable construction solutions for years to come.
