May 26, 2021

PHPC Corporate Social Responsibility Activity 2
Hoyo Elementary School



Despite of the pandemic we are facing today, which is caused by the Coronavirus (also known as COVID-19), the executives of PHPC Co., Ltd. Inc. planned and discussed agendas on how to execute supportive actions that would help in some ways our Filipino citizens especially at this moment in time as essential part also of our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR).

The company communicated with DepEd to help us look for schools who most need our assistance and help. From Metro Manila, our team travelled to Silang, Cavite to visit Hoyo Elementary School, one of the beneficiaries. We have donated computers sets that will surely be helpful for the faculty.

Our company aims to continue this kind of program and to widen our scope not just by merely practicing our CSR but having the heart to continue serving and helping people in any way that we can.
