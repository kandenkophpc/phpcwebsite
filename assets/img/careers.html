Project Manager

   Duties and Responsibilities

  • Directs the activities of the Project to achieve efficiency and effectiveness in the management of construction projects including construction change orders, warranty and the like.
  • Over-all administration of Project Management and Construction activities and resources. Directs through its subordinates, the planning, scheduling, coordination and monitoring of various construction activities and resources including quality, environmental, safety and health issues
  • Monitors project cost/s for financial planning, and provide guidance for preparation of project budget and the allocation of available resources.
  • Conducts management studies pertaining to the development of new programs, operations or work methods; monitors and evaluates objectives and program procedures.
  • Liaise with clients, suppliers and subcontractors to ensure delivery of quality work.
  • Performs other related work as required.

Knowledge,Abilities and Skills

  • Thorough knowledge of the principles and practices of project management and construction.
  • Thorough knowledge of all government and local regulatory requirements applicable to project management and construction.
  • Considerable knowledge of the funding processes related to budgeting, cost accounting, financial planning and management.
  • Considerable knowledge of the principles, practices and procedures of supervision, organization and administration.
  • Ability to plan, implement, and coordinate technical and administrative activities.
  • Ability to express ideas effectively, both orally and in writing.
  • Ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships with the general public, co-workers, elected and appointed officials and members of diverse cultural and linguistic backgrounds.
  • Highly analytical and with good planning skills.
  • Computer literate.

Job Qualifications

  • Preferably licensed Engineer
  • 40 to 50 years old
  • With at least 18 years related work experience.
  • With 7 years Managerial experience
  • With Masteral degree an advantage
  • With extensive knowledge on the following:
    • HV / LV Power and Distribution System
    • Lighting and Convenience Outlet System
    • Building Management System
    • Auxiliary System
    • Building Management System
    • Lightning Protection and Grounding System
    • HVAC
    • Plumbing
    • Ducting
    • Piping
    • Other related mechanical works

ASSISTANT PURCHASING MANAGER

Job Responsibilities

  • Manage the entire process in the planning of procurement and supply chain activities, inventory control, logistics and distribution and ensure effectively functioning processes to avoid costly delays and lost opportunities. Plan, direct, organize and control the work of Purchasing Department.
  • Perform all procurement activities including pre-qualification, negotiation of supplier agreements, preparation of contracts and tender management.
  • Ensure full compliance of procurement implementation of policies and activities with Company ethic rules and ISO.
  • Thoroughly understand the required materials specifications and service needs of the Company and may delegate responsibilities to staff for routing purchases under strict guidance.
  • Control all purchases of equipment, materials and construction and administrative services based on the approved requests by the different Departments of the Company. In charge to control major purchases and make certain that the procurement of materials and services comply strictly with the Purchasing guidelines.
  • Establish and manage procurement database, including information of vendors/suppliers, price records, evaluation rate, technical information and all other necessary information needed. Establishes reliable supply sources and back-ups to ensure uninterrupted project sites and departmental work activities.
  • Formulate control procedure as necessary in order to ensure that purchase of goods and services are for the best interest of the Company as well as formulate back-up procedure/s to avoid disruption of construction works at site.
  • Responsible for introducing improvements in the supply chain and identify new vendors/suppliers without jeopardizing compliance to Company rules and procedures.
  • Manage and supervise the operation (inventory, safekeeping, and issuance) of the equipment and materials in the main warehouse area. Evaluate inventory at the warehouse and recommend disposal of scrap materials as needed.
  • Reports to Management any material movement of prices and significant movement in the market affecting the operations of the Company. Oversees the updates with the new materials and techniques; recommend more cost-effective measures to Engineering, Project sites and other departments.
  • Search for competitive bids and award contracts based on Company’s profitability, emphasizing quality and ability to deliver accordingly.
  • Performs other related work as required.

Qualifications

  • Graduate of any Bachelor’s degree. With minimum of 5 years’ experience in material, construction, or general service procurement.
  • Must possess highest degree of integrity and professionalism; mature; analytical; goal oriented and with strong negotiation skills; strong communicator in English and can relate with foreign and local counterparts.
  • Computer literate and with knowledge in data base management.
